Bio

Timothy (Tim) Flavin is a Ph.D. Candidate in the MASTERS lab at TU specializing in multi-agent reinforcement learning (MARL) for ad hoc teamwork. His work focuses on designing intelligent agents capable of dynamic coordination without prior collaboration, bridging the gap between theoretical advancements and practical deployment in complex environments. With a both industry and academic experience in machine learning, stochastic modeling, web development, game engine development, and aircraft control design, Timothy applies a multidisciplinary approach to problem-solving. His expertise extends from designing adaptive AI systems to developing robust real-time applications. Beyond research, he enjoys skiing, rock climbing, and game nights with friends, embracing both intellectual and physical challenges in equal measure.
